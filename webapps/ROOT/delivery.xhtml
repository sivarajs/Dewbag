Delivery

Customers who order online can choose the desired slots for the delivery. There are four delivery slots available for our customers at dewbag.com.

Four slots are available for the delivery

  • 7 AM to 9 AM
  • 10 AM to 12 PM
  • 3 PM to 5 PM
  • 7 PM to 9 PM

Customers can choose one of the convenient slots to deliver their ordered product during checkout. Customers can also checkout by choosing desired date arrange (7days) and time for their delivery.
