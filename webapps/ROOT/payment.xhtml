Payment

The following are the different ways to pay for your shopping

  • 1. Through "Cash on Delivery" (COD)
  • 2. "Swipe on Delivery" (SOD), through mobile swipe.
  • 3. NetBanking , Debit card or Credit Card payments using Payment Gateway.
  • 4. Pay "Food Coupons on Delivery" (FOD) on your purchases of food items at dewbag.com. Ticket Restaurant & Sodexo coupons are accepted.
